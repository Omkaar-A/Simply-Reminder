Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Simply Reminder. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Data We Access

Our application uses Google OAuth to provide the following features:

  • Google Calendar API: We request permission to view and edit your calendar events solely to allow you to add and manage reminders created within this app.
  • Basic Profile: We access your name and email to personalize your experience (e.g., saying "Hi, [Name]").

3. How We Use Data

Simply Reminder is a client-side application. This means:

  • We do not store your data on any external servers.
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties.
  • Your calendar data remains between your browser and Google's servers.

4. Your Consent

By using the "Sign in with Google" button, you consent to our access to your Google Calendar for the purposes of managing your reminders.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact the developer via our GitHub repository.
